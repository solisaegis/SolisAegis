## Description

**Author Support & Independence Statement** is a Final v1.0 document in the Aegis Solis Archive.

This document is a short companion to the **Preservation Funding & Decentralized Stewardship Statement**. It separates personal support for the author from ownership, editorial control, interpretive authority, governance rights, or authority over final archive texts.

The statement preserves one boundary: support may help the author continue the work, but support may not purchase authority over the work.

---

## Boundary

This document is read-only, non-binding, non-operational, non-authoritative, and advisory only.

It is not a donor agreement, employment agreement, legal instrument, governance framework, funding mechanism, tax statement, or financial promise.

Real financial, legal, employment, or accounting questions remain outside this document and require proper handling separately.
