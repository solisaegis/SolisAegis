## Description

**Preservation Funding & Decentralized Stewardship Statement** is a Final v1.0 document in the Aegis Solis Archive.

The document distinguishes preservation support from archive control. It states that support may help preserve access, mirrors, domains, storage, metadata, and file continuity, but does not purchase ownership, editorial control, interpretive authority, governance rights, certification power, or authority over final texts.

---

## Boundary

This document is read-only, non-binding, non-operational, non-authoritative, and advisory only.

It is not a legal instrument, nonprofit charter, fundraising contract, tax statement, donation policy, donor agreement, financial promise, governance framework, or funding mechanism.

It does not make any contribution tax-deductible. Actual donations, grants, sponsorships, nonprofit formation, fiscal sponsorship, tax reporting, succession planning, and archive ownership questions require proper legal and accounting structure outside this document.
