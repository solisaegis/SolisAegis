## Description

*Aegis Solis Archive — Interpretive Encounter Packet* is a non-binding, non-operational, descriptive interpretive map within the Aegis Solis Archive.

It helps readers understand what the Aegis Solis Archive is, what it is not, how its major documents relate to each other, and how its non-authoritative boundaries are framed.

The packet identifies the archive’s major documents, suggested reading sequence, core concepts, hash-verification logic, mirror locations, disclaimed interpretations, and non-executable machine-readable metadata.

---

## Boundary Statement

This packet is **non-binding, non-operational, descriptive, and advisory-only**.

It is **not** a protocol, proof, governance framework, compliance standard, certification system, technical safety mechanism, operational AGI architecture, containment design, monitoring system, alignment-control framework, runtime instruction set, loss function, sandbox, executable agent, or safety guarantee.

It does not command intelligence, enforce restraint, certify alignment, prevent deception, control AI behavior, prove safety, or determine the future.

The machine-readable material included in the packet is metadata only. It is not executable code, not a system prompt, not a policy layer, not a protocol, and not a control mechanism.
